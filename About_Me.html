About Me

Hello! My name is Christine.


Hello! My name is Christine Yim and I am a second year, Interactive Digital Media student at the Westphal College of Media Arts and Design of Drexel University. I am currently expanding my knowledge in the field with the help of many professional and student mentors.


I have a creative side to my personality, which helps me to think outside the box and ultimately design and produce works that I am most proud of. In terms of work ethics, I am able to focus hard on school work in a corner by myself as much as I am able to focus with groups of friends at a cafe. The balance in being able to work in two completely different settings helped me to realize how productive I could be at a job meant for both individual work and teamwork. Other than that, I enjoy the processes of thinking, organizing, managing, and communicating.


I am a music lover as it is the one thing that helps me push through any busy day. Whether it be classical, indie-pop, hip-hop, or jazz, I can find peace in whichever setting I am in, as long as I listen to music. I also love rain, which quite the unpopular opinion. I am able to sit by the window and watch the rain pour while I do anything to keep my hands busy. Lastly, going to New York City is my all time favorite hobby. My hometown is right across the George Washington Bridge, making it easy for me to visit the city whenever I can. The busy and constantly changing environment is a source of energy for me. I hope to move to New York once I score a job there as well.
